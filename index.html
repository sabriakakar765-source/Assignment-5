About Me

I am an English instructor and computer specialist working at the intersection of education, technology, and creativity. I believe learning becomes meaningful when it is combined with real-world experience and modern tools. In the field of education, I strive to present concepts in a simple, clear, and inspiring way so that learners not only understand, but also gain the confidence to apply their knowledge. In the world of technology, my focus is on web design and development — where ideas are transformed into digital experiences. Using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I create projects that are both visually engaging and functionally effective. I pay close attention to detail, I am committed to continuous growth, and I am always driven to build something beyond the ordinary. My goal is to create a lasting impact through knowledge, creativity, and technology.
